How I Work

A repeatable loop — not a waterfall.

Understand

Stakeholder interviews, user research, competitive audit. I enter every project with questions, not answers.

Frame

Opportunity definition, problem framing, success metrics. Strategy before screens.

Explore

Divergent ideation, lo-fi flows, rapid iteration. I sketch ugly before I design beautiful.

Validate

Prototype testing, usability studies, data synthesis. Decisions earn their place with evidence.

Ship & Learn

Developer handoff, QA partnership, post-launch analytics. Done means live — not handed off.
